About DRAMSOC

DRAMSOC is the premier drama society at the University of Nairobi, dedicated to promoting theatrical excellence and creative expression.

Our Mission

We strive to:

  • Foster a vibrant theater community
  • Provide platforms for creative expression
  • Develop theatrical talent and skills
  • Promote diversity and inclusion in the arts
  • Create memorable experiences for students and audiences

Our History

DRAMSOC was founded with the vision of bringing theatrical excellence to the University of Nairobi. Since its inception, we have produced numerous acclaimed productions and nurtured countless theatrical talents.

From our humble beginnings to becoming one of the most dynamic societies on campus, DRAMSOC continues to evolve while maintaining its commitment to quality theater.

Our Values

Creativity

We believe in the power of creative expression and encourage all members to explore their artistic potential.

Collaboration

Theater is a collaborative art form. We foster teamwork and mutual support among all members.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering high-quality productions that showcase the best of theatrical talent.

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone, regardless of experience level, to join our community and pursue their passion for theater.

Innovation

We constantly explore new ideas and techniques to keep our productions fresh and engaging.

Community

We are committed to being an integral part of the university community and serving our audiences.
