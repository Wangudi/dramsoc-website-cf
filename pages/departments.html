Our Departments

DRAMSOC operates through specialized departments, each dedicated to a crucial aspect of theatrical production.

Departments

Acting

Development and training of acting skills, scene work, and performance techniques.

Directing

Director training, vision development, and leadership in theatrical productions.

Production

Stage management, logistics, and overall production coordination for all shows.

Technical

Lighting, sound engineering, and technical support for theatrical performances.

Costume & Makeup

Costume design, creation, makeup artistry, and wardrobe management.

Set Design

Scenic design, set construction, and visual environment creation.

Administration

Governance, finance management, and organizational operations.
