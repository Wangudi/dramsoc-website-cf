About DRAMSOC

The Strathmore Drama Society (DRAMSOC) is the premier theatrical organization at Strathmore University, dedicated to bringing compelling stories to life through the art of drama and performance.

Our Mission

To nurture theatrical talent and create unforgettable performances that inspire, entertain, and provoke thought within our university community and beyond.

Our Vision

To be the leading university drama society in Kenya, recognized for excellence in theatrical production and artist development.

Our Values

Creativity, collaboration, excellence, and inclusivity drive everything we do, from auditions to final curtain calls.
